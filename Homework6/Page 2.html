Favorite Story

My favorite story is /The Road/ by Cormac McCarthy. It's a 2006
sci-fi nuclear-fallout novel that follows two unnamed characters
(The Boy and The Man) as they travel to the ocean.
All living creatures and plants have died in nuclear fallout,
and most humans have resorted to cannibalism.
/The Road/ is concerned with hope in the face of despair,
the question, "What do we owe each other?", and impermanence.
